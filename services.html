Services & Products





What products and services are provided by DGtronicz®?


DGtronicz® functions as an ICT Consultancy which provides services to both residential and commericial clients.

In addition to these services, DGtronicz® also retails computer peripherals, computer accessories and mobile phone accesories.


Services


As an IT Consultancy, DGtronicz® provides services to residential and commercial clients. These services include but are not limited to:

  • Web Development: Web Design & Hosting
  • PC hardware repair & maintenance
  • Wired & Wireless Networking
  • Technical Support
  • Android Support
  • Software Installation & Maintenance


Products


DGtronicz® also retails computer peripherals, mobile accessories and PC accessories. These include but are not limited to:

  • USB 3.0 and USB 3.1 Flash Drives
  • PC mouse devices for gaming
  • Micro SD cards
  • Mobile phone cases
  • Mobile phone screen protectors
