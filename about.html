Who or what is DGtronicz®?


DGtronicz® (pronounced: "di-gee-tron-icks") is a micro-enterprise business in the industry of Information & Communications Technology (ICT), which was founded in 2012 by Mr. Damien C.W. Griffith.

The business functions as an ICT Consultancy, providing services to both residential households and commercial companies.

Mr. Griffith functions as the Managing Director and ICT Consultant.
